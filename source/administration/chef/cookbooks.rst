Cookbooks
===========

`Chef cookbooks available in (chef-repo) <https://www.github.com/megamsys/chef-repo>`__ .

Requirements
============

The hosts must have a working installation of Chef, that usually requires:

-  Ruby > 1.9.3, preferred 2.1.1 
-  Chef-Server >= 11.x
